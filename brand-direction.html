02 // The Rule That Keeps It Premium

Green leads. Orange pops. Dark grounds it.

Green = the brand

Headlines, the logo, the main accents. It's the color people associate with you. Growth + money.

Orange = one pop

ONE orange thing per graphic — a button, a keyword, the CTA. Restraint is what makes it look designed, not Halloween.

Dark = the ground

Near-black with a faint green tint. Lets both colors glow and keeps the whole thing premium.
